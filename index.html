What is Montessori Inspired Curriculum?

Hands-on learning combined with 21st-century STEAM curriculum.

At Karter Schools, the Cascades campus is presented as a Montessori-inspired classroom experience that integrates the principles of Maria Montessori with Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics.

Montessori Foundation

Hands-on Montessori materials empower students to explore and learn through tactile experiences, building a deep understanding of foundational concepts.

STEAM Integration

By merging time-tested methods with a forward-looking STEAM approach, students build creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills for an evolving world.

Karter Schools believes in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically proficient, but also equipped to excel in our ever-evolving world.

This balanced model combines confidence, curiosity, and strong academic readiness in one classroom journey.
