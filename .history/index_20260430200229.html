What Is Montessori Inspired Curriculum?

Hands-on learning combined with 21st-century STEAM curriculum.

At Karter Schools, the Cascades campus is presented as a Montessori-inspired classroom experience that integrates the principles of Maria Montessori with Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics.

The campus page explains that hands-on Montessori materials empower students to explore and learn through tactile experiences, helping them build a deeper understanding of foundational concepts.

By combining time-tested Montessori methods with a forward-looking STEAM approach, Karter positions the program around creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

Karter Schools states that it believes in nurturing well-rounded individuals who are academically proficient and equipped to excel in an ever-evolving world.
